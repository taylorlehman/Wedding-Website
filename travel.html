Travel

James Madison’s Montpelier is located in Orange, VA - about 30 minutes northeast from the town of Charlottesville, and about an hour and a half from Washington DC. Depending on your travel preferences, you can stay in any of these locations.

We have reserved hotel blocks at the Holiday Inn Express in Orange, which is about 10 minutes from Montpelier, as well as the Courtyard by Marriott in Charlottesville, about 30 minutes from Montpelier. There are also a number of B&Bs and inns around the Charlottesville and Montpelier area
