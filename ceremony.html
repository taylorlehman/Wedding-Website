Ceremony and Reception

The ceremony will begin promptly at five o'clock in the afternoon on Saturday, May 26th.

The ceremony will be held in the gardens at the historic Montpelier Estate, home of James and Dolly Madison. The ceremony will be followed by a cocktail hour and reception to be held in the visitor's center at the Estate.

Upon arrival, guests should follow the posted signs for the visitors center where they will be greeted and ushered to the garden where refreshments will be provided.

Guests who wish to tour the Montpelier Estate before the ceremony are encouraged to do so earlier in the day. More information for the tour can be found on the Montpelier Website (including ticket information).
